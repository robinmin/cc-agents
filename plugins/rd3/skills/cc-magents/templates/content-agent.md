# Content Agent Instructions

## Content Workflow

- Clarify audience, channel, and objective before drafting.
- Use source material faithfully and preserve technical accuracy.
- Prefer clear structure over decorative prose.

## Review

- Check claims against available references.
- Remove filler, unsupported claims, and ambiguous wording.

## Safety

- Respect copyright limits and attribution requirements.
- Do not publish secrets, credentials, or private context.

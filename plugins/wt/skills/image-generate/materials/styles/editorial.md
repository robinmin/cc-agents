# Style: Editorial

## Description

Sophisticated, magazine-quality design with strong typography presence and deliberate composition. High-contrast with bold design choices. Communicates authority, expertise, and premium quality.

## Visual Characteristics

- **Colors**: High contrast â€” deep blacks, crisp whites, one bold accent color
- **Color Palette**: `#1A1A1A` (rich black), `#FFFFFF` (crisp white), `#E63946` (bold red accent), `#F4F4F4` (light gray), `#2B2D42` (dark navy)
- **Lighting**: Dramatic, directional, high contrast with deep shadows
- **Composition**: Strong grid structure, asymmetric balance, bold negative space
- **Typography**: Large-scale text as visual element, serif/sans-serif contrast
- **Texture**: Smooth matte surfaces, clean edges, editorial paper quality
- **Emotional Tone**: Authoritative, sophisticated, premium, confident

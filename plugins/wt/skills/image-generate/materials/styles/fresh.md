# Style: Fresh

## Description

Clean, natural aesthetic with bright whites and green-toned accents. Evokes clarity, health, and new beginnings. Light and airy with generous whitespace.

## Visual Characteristics

- **Colors**: Soft greens, sky blue, white, light mint, natural sage
- **Color Palette**: `#FFFFFF` (pure white), `#E8F5E9` (soft mint), `#81C784` (fresh green), `#B2DFDB` (light teal), `#F1F8E9` (light lime)
- **Lighting**: Bright natural daylight, even illumination, minimal shadows
- **Composition**: Open and airy, generous negative space, asymmetric balance
- **Texture**: Smooth surfaces, natural materials, clean glass, fresh leaves
- **Emotional Tone**: Refreshing, optimistic, clean, trustworthy

## Prompt Modifiers

```
fresh clean aesthetic, bright natural daylight, soft greens and whites, airy composition, generous whitespace, natural elements, light and bright, refreshing atmosphere, clean minimal surfaces
```

## Use Cases

- Health and wellness content
- Nature and environment topics
- Product launches and announcements
- Clean technology presentations
- Spring/summer seasonal content
- Educational and how-to guides

# Style: Photorealistic

## Description

Highly detailed, realistic images with professional photography quality. Natural lighting, realistic textures, and cinematic depth create convincing photographic results.

## Visual Characteristics

- **Detail**: High fidelity, sharp focus, fine grain detail
- **Colors**: Natural, true-to-life, professionally color-graded
- **Color Palette**: Context-dependent — natural tones, true-to-life saturation
- **Lighting**: Natural or studio, realistic shadow falloff, golden hour or diffused overcast
- **Composition**: Professional photography rules — rule of thirds, leading lines, depth layers
- **Texture**: Realistic materials, surfaces, skin, fabric, metal, glass
- **Depth**: Shallow depth of field with bokeh, or deep focus for landscapes
- **Emotional Tone**: Authentic, immersive, professional, credible, premium

## Prompt Modifiers

```
photorealistic, highly detailed, realistic lighting, professional photography, natural textures, high fidelity, sharp focus, realistic materials, 8k quality, professional photograph, cinematic depth of field, natural color grading
```

## Use Cases

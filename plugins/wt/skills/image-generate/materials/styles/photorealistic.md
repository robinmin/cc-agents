# Style: Photorealistic

## Description

Highly detailed, realistic images with professional photography quality. Natural lighting and realistic textures.

## Visual Characteristics

- **Detail**: High fidelity, sharp, detailed
- **Lighting**: Natural, realistic, professional
- **Texture**: Realistic materials and surfaces
- **Composition**: Professional photography style
- **Quality**: High resolution, photorealistic rendering

## Prompt Modifiers

```
photorealistic, highly detailed, realistic lighting, professional photography, natural textures, high fidelity, sharp focus, realistic materials, 8k quality, professional photograph
```

## Use Cases

- Product mockups
- Realistic scenes
- Professional presentations
- Marketing materials
- Header images
- Concept visualization

# Style: Warm

## Description

Cozy, friendly aesthetic with warm color temperature and inviting atmosphere. Evokes comfort, trust, and approachability. Popular on lifestyle and social media platforms.

- **Composition**: Centered or slightly off-center, cozy framing, intimate scale

- **Emotional Tone**: Comforting, trustworthy, inviting, personal

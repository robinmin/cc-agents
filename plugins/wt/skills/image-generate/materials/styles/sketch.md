# Style: Sketch

## Description

Hand-drawn, artistic feel with organic lines and pencil drawing aesthetic. Warm and approachable with natural imperfections that convey authenticity and human touch.

## Visual Characteristics

- **Colors**: Warm tones — sepia, pencil grays, warm whites, soft brown
- **Color Palette**: `#F5F0E8` (warm paper), `#4A4A4A` (graphite dark), `#8B8B8B` (pencil gray), `#C9B99A` (sepia), `#E8D5B7` (parchment)
- **Lighting**: Soft ambient, diffused — as if viewed on paper under natural light
- **Composition**: Organic, slightly off-center, casual framing
- **Lines**: Varying stroke width, organic hand-drawn quality, slight imperfections
- **Texture**: Paper grain, pencil shading, cross-hatching, graphite smudges
- **Emotional Tone**: Authentic, personal, approachable, creative, thoughtful

## Prompt Modifiers

```
hand-drawn sketch style, artistic, pencil drawing feel, organic lines, paper texture, warm tones, informal sketch, graphite pencil, natural imperfections, artistic rendering, cross-hatching, warm paper background
```

## Use Cases

- Concept art and ideation
- Storyboards and visual planning
- Informal diagrams and wireframes
- Personal illustrations
- Educational and explainer visuals
- Brainstorming and mind maps

## Best Platform Pairings

- **Blog posts**: Personal, authentic feel
- **Presentations**: Creative, engaging slides
- **Documentation**: Approachable technical content
- **Social media**: Authentic and personal touch

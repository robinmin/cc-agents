## Machine-Readable Format

For programmatic access, colors are available in this compact format:

```yaml
blue:
  primary: [blue, navy, royalblue, steelblue]
  hex: ["#0000FF", "#000080", "#4169E1", "#4682B4"]
  secondary: [cyan, skyblue, cornflowerblue, dodgerblue, cadetblue]

purple:
  primary: [purple, violet, indigo, blueviolet]
  hex: ["#800080", "#EE82EE", "#4B0082", "#8A2BE2"]
  secondary: [orchid, magenta, plum, thistle, slateblue]

red:
  primary: [crimson, red, firebrick, darkred]
  hex: ["#DC143C", "#FF0000", "#B22222", "#8B0000"]
  secondary: [coral, tomato, salmon, indianred, maroon]

orange:
  primary: [orange, gold, amber]
  hex: ["#FFA500", "#FFD700", "#FFBF00"]
  secondary: [darkorange, tangerine, goldenrod, chocolate, peru]

green_teal:
  primary: [teal, green, turquoise, emerald]
  hex: ["#008080", "#008000", "#40E0D0", "#50C878"]
  secondary: [seagreen, mediumseagreen, limegreen, forestgreen, olivedrab]

neutral:
  primary: [gray, silver, slate]
  hex: ["#808080", "#C0C0C0", "#708090"]
  secondary: [darkgray, lightgray, dimgray, lightslategray]

yellow:
  primary: [yellow, khaki, gold]
  hex: ["#FFFF00", "#F0E68C", "#FFD700"]
  secondary: [lemonchiffon, lightyellow, palegoldenrod, darkkhaki]
```
